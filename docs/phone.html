View Caster

Set up once, then stream from your glasses.

Step 1 — Connect Meta AI

Add View Caster to your glasses through the Meta AI app.

  1. Meta AI opens on your phone
  2. Go to Devices → your Display glasses
  3. App connections → Web apps → Add
  4. Name: View Caster, paste the URL above, tap Connect
  5. Open View Caster on your glasses (you’ll enter a code there later)

The glasses URL is copied when you tap Open Meta AI.

Step 2 — Camera for live view

Live Stream uses your phone camera to cast to the desktop (glasses web apps can’t access the camera directly).

Allow camera so Live Stream can cast to your desktop.
